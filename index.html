Whispers of My Soul

Assurance and Understanding

You always give me the assurance that I needed whenever I am anxious about something. You always tell me that everything is going to be alright. You are also the most understanding person that I have met and despite of my short-comings, you still always choose to love me.

Articulate and Well-versed

You always know what to say in certain situations, especially whenever we fight, you always know how to make me understand why you felt that way. You also know how to read the room and I love how insightful to talk to you even we are gossiping about the things that are happening in our life.

Unconditional Love

I cherish our unconditional love for each other because even though we have our own shortcomings, our own drawbacks, our own imperfections, we have that love and bond for each other that would never break no matter what happens. Even if we have our own battles, we have each other's backs and support each other. That's something that I adore that we have and something that I'll treasure till I die.

Shall I compare thee to a summer's day?

Thou art more lovely and more temperate.

Rough winds do shake the darling buds of May,

And summer's lease hath all too short a date.

Sometime too hot the eye of heaven shines,

And often is his gold complexion dimmed;

And every fair from fair sometime declines,

By chance, or nature's changing course, untrimmed;

-William Shakespeare
